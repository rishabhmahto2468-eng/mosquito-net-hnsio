​Privacy Policy

**1. What Info We Grab**

We might grab a couple of different types of info:

a. Stuff That IDs You

This is stuff that tells us who you are, things like:

*   Your full name
*   Your email address
*   Your phone number
*   Where to send stuff/ your billing address
*   Payment info (Don't worry, our payment people handle this safely.)
*   Anything you type into our forms.

b. Other Info

This is auto-collected data that doesn't really ID you, such as:

*   Your IP address
*   What browser and device you're using
*   What pages you looked at, how long you stayed; and where you came from
*   Roughly where you're located

We might use cookies and other tech to get all of this info.

**2. Why We Snag This Info**

We use this data to:

*   Keep making our stuff better
*   Process orders
*   Answer questions
*   Send you important updates like policy changes
*   See how we're doing
*   Follow the law.

**3. Legal Stuff (If It Matters to You)**

If you're in Europe, we only use your info if:

*   You said it was okay
*   We need it to fulfill our deal
*   The law says we have to
*   We have a good reason (like making stuff better).

**4. Who We Share Info With**

We might share your info with:

*   People who help us with the website, payments, etc.
*   Our advisors.
*   If the law requires it, with authorities.

We do not sell or rent your personal info to anyone.

**5. How Long We Keep It**

We only keep your info as long as we need it to do what we said or as long as the law requires. After that we'll delete it

**6. How We Keep Your Info Safe**

We're going to safeguard to keep your info safe from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Just keep in mind that no system is perfect.

**7. International Transfers**

Your info might end up in another country. We'll comply with all the data protection laws and make sure your info's safe.

**8. Your Rights**

Depending on where you live, you might:

*   Be able to see your data
*   Change or delete your data
*   Take back your consent
*   Put a stop certain things with your data
*   Reach out and talk about it to the data authorities/ put in a complaint

**9. Cookies**

We use cookies to:

*   Remember your settings
*   Make the website easier to use
*   See what's popular on the site

You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

**10. Links to Other Sites**

We might link to other websites, but we're not responsible for their stuff. Read their privacy policies.

**11. Policy Changes**

We might change this policy, from time to time. The latest version will be posted here with a new 'Last Updated' date. If you keep using the site, that means you accept the changes.
